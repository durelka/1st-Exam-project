My Career So Far

It is always difficult to start something new, despite this I am slowly ,but surely developing a "business" with clay pots through Instagram, which I personally produce in Estonia. Learn more about it here.
After finishing art school and graduating from high school, I decided to move to Denmark, where, at the moment, I am studying in Zealand (Køge, Denmark) in the direction of Multimedia design and earning a bachelor's degree. However, I have not yet had time to try myself in the role of a multimedia designer outside the university or as a cross / transmedia specialist after completing the courses in the Tallinn University (Tallinn, Estonia).
